# Paulin-Mohring: Inductive Definitions in the System Coq (1993)

## Citation
Paulin-Mohring, C. "Inductive Definitions in the System Coq — Rules and Properties."
In Proceedings of the International Conference on Typed Lambda Calculi and Applications
(TLCA), pp. 328–345. LNCS. Springer-Verlag, March 1993.

## Key Contributions
- Detailed presentation of the rules for inductive definitions as implemented in Coq
- Properties of the extended system including subject reduction
- Treatment of pattern matching and fixpoint definitions
- Specification of the guard condition for structural recursion

## Context
- Extension of Paulin-Mohring's earlier PhD thesis (1989, Université Paris VII)
  "Extraction de programmes dans le Calcul des Constructions"
- This paper provides the definitive reference for the CIC as implemented in Coq
