# Benjamin Pierce's Subtyping-Related Bibliography

Source: https://www.cis.upenn.edu/~bcpierce/papers/subtyping-bib.html

## Papers

1. **"Bounded Quantification is Undecidable"** (1994)
   - Authors: Benjamin C. Pierce
   - Venue: Information and Computation, vol. 112, no. 1
   - Summary at POPL 1992, Albuquerque, New Mexico

2. **"Decidable Bounded Quantification"** (1994)
   - Authors: Giuseppe Castagna and Benjamin Pierce
   - Venue: ACM SIGPLAN-SIGACT Symposium on Principles of Programming Languages (POPL), Portland, Oregon

3. **"Higher-Order Subtyping"** (1994)
   - Authors: Benjamin C. Pierce and Martin Steffen
   - Venue: IFIP Working Conference on Programming Concepts, Methods and Calculi (PROCOMET)
   - Full version in Theoretical Computer Science, 1997

4. **"Corrigendum: Decidable Bounded Quantification"** (1995)
   - Authors: Giuseppe Castagna and Benjamin Pierce
   - Venue: POPL 1995

5. **"Bounded Quantification with Bottom"** (1997)
   - Author: Benjamin C. Pierce
   - Venue: Technical Report, Indiana University

6. **"Bounded Existentials and Minimal Typing"** (1992/1998)
   - Authors: Giorgio Ghelli and Benjamin Pierce
   - Venue: Theoretical Computer Science

7. **"Recursive Subtyping Revealed"** (2003)
   - Authors: Vladimir Gapeyev, Michael Levin, and Benjamin Pierce
   - Venue: Journal of Functional Programming, vol. 12, no. 6

8. **"On Decidability of Nominal Subtyping with Variance"** (2006)
   - Authors: Andrew J. Kennedy and Benjamin C. Pierce
   - Venue: FOOL-WOOD '07

9. **"Datatypes and Subtyping"** (1998)
   - Authors: Haruo Hosoya, Benjamin C. Pierce, and David N. Turner
   - Venue: Unpublished manuscript
