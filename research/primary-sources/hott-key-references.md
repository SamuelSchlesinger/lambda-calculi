# HoTT and Cubical Type Theory: Key Primary Sources

## Foundational Papers and Books

1. **The HoTT Book**: The Univalent Foundations Program, "Homotopy Type Theory: Univalent Foundations of Mathematics," Institute for Advanced Study, 2013.
   - URL: https://homotopytypetheory.org/book/
   - ArXiv: https://arxiv.org/abs/1308.0729

2. **Hofmann-Streicher Groupoid Model**: Martin Hofmann and Thomas Streicher, "The groupoid interpretation of type theory," in *Twenty-five years of constructive type theory* (Venice, 1995), Oxford Logic Guides, vol. 36, Oxford University Press, 1998, pp. 83-111.
   - URL: https://ncatlab.org/nlab/files/HofmannStreicherGroupoidInterpretation.pdf

3. **Awodey-Warren**: Steve Awodey and Michael Warren, "Homotopy theoretic models of identity types," *Mathematical Proceedings of the Cambridge Philosophical Society*, 2009.
   - ArXiv: https://arxiv.org/abs/0709.0248

4. **Voevodsky's Origins**: Vladimir Voevodsky, "The Origins and Motivations of Univalent Foundations," IAS, 2014.
   - URL: https://www.ias.edu/ideas/2014/voevodsky-origins

## Cubical Type Theory

5. **CCHM**: Cyril Cohen, Thierry Coquand, Simon Huber, Anders Mörtberg, "Cubical Type Theory: a constructive interpretation of the univalence axiom," *TYPES 2015*, published 2018.
   - ArXiv: https://arxiv.org/abs/1611.02108
   - PDF: https://www.cse.chalmers.se/~coquand/cubicaltt.pdf

6. **Cartesian Cubical Type Theory (ABCFHL)**: Carlo Angiuli, Guillaume Brunerie, Thierry Coquand, Kuen-Bang Hou (Favonia), Robert Harper, Daniel R. Licata, "Syntax and Models of Cartesian Cubical Type Theory," 2017.
   - PDF: https://www.cs.cmu.edu/~rwh/papers/uniform/uniform.pdf

7. **Cubical Agda**: Andrea Vezzosi, Anders Mörtberg, Andreas Abel, "Cubical Agda: a dependently typed programming language with univalence and higher inductive types," *ICFP 2019*.
   - PDF: https://staff.math.su.se/anders.mortberg/papers/cubicalagda2.pdf
   - Agda docs: https://agda.readthedocs.io/en/latest/language/cubical.html

## Identity Types and Synthetic Homotopy Theory

8. **Licata-Shulman pi_1(S^1)**: Daniel R. Licata and Michael Shulman, "Calculating the Fundamental Group of the Circle in Homotopy Type Theory," *LICS 2013*.
   - PDF: https://www.cs.cmu.edu/~drl/pubs/ls13pi1s1/ls13pi1s1.pdf
   - ArXiv: https://arxiv.org/abs/1301.3443

9. **Brunerie pi_4(S^3)**: Axel Ljungström and Anders Mörtberg, "Formalising π₄(S³) ≅ ℤ/2ℤ and Computing a Brunerie Number in Cubical Agda," 2023.
   - ArXiv: https://arxiv.org/abs/2302.00151

## Observational Type Theory

10. **OTT**: Thorsten Altenkirch, Conor McBride, Wouter Swierstra, "Observational Equality, Now!" *PLPV 2007*.
    - PDF: https://people.cs.nott.ac.uk/psztxa/publ/obseqnow.pdf
