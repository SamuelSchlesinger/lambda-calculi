# Barendregt, H.P. "Lambda Calculi with Types."

**Citation:** Barendregt, H.P. "Lambda Calculi with Types." In *Handbook of Logic in
Computer Science*, Volume 2, eds. S. Abramsky, D.M. Gabbay, T.S.E. Maibaum,
Oxford University Press, 1992.

**PDF available at:** https://home.ttic.edu/~dreyer/course/papers/barendregt.pdf

## Summary

This is Barendregt's comprehensive survey of typed lambda calculi, including the lambda
cube, Pure Type Systems, and their properties. It is one of the most cited references
in type theory.

### Key Topics Covered

1. **Simply Typed Lambda Calculus** (lambda-arrow): Church and Curry versions
2. **System F** (lambda-2): Girard's polymorphic lambda calculus
3. **The Lambda Cube**: All eight systems with formal definitions
4. **Pure Type Systems**: Full formalization with typing rules
5. **Properties**: Strong normalization, Church-Rosser, subject reduction
6. **Curry-Howard Correspondence**: Connections between type systems and logics

### Historical Significance

This handbook chapter provides the most complete and authoritative treatment of the
lambda cube and Pure Type Systems. It establishes the standard notation and terminology
used throughout the field.
